8 Petal Origami Flower by Grace Austen

8 Petal Origami Flower Instructions

This model I actually designed and made the instructions for myself. I am happy with the solution I found to getting another four flaps to make petals from. The model is not too hard to fold except for step 25. Most of the instructions is prep-work for that step. Step 25 is a little bit tricky to execute and I am unsure if I made it apparent what was happening.
